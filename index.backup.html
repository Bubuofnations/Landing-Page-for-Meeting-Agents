FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to the questions you're probably already thinking.

Does Meeting AI store or record audio? +

No. Meeting AI never stores or records audio. Transcription happens in real time and only the text transcript is processed. Audio never leaves your device.

Which platforms does it work on? +

Meeting AI works on Google Meet, Zoom web, and Microsoft Teams web — running in Chrome on a desktop. If you're using browser-based meetings, you're good to go.

How fast does Meeting AI surface insights? +

Within seconds of detecting a relevant moment — a risk, an alignment gap, a direct question. The goal is to give you enough time to think and respond, not a post-meeting summary.

How is this different from Otter.ai or Fireflies? +

Those tools summarize after your meetings end. Meeting AI acts during them — surfacing risks, alignment gaps, and telling you what to say while the conversation is still happening. The value is in the moment, not the recap.

Is there a free plan? +

Yes — up to 3 meetings, no credit card required. Enough to decide whether Meeting AI is worth it before you commit to anything.
