I am Busayo and I work at a company where things move very fast, and it isn't happening in isolation. Every company wants to innovate or be the first to the market. As someone who expects so much from himself, it sometimes feels like it's hard to catch up. 
* Wake up in the morning, do some focused work.
* Go to meetings, do some focused work.
* Try to go into another meeting, another meeting, and another meeting, and try to do some other work.
* Still have to prepare for a presentation the next day, and so on.
* Working on several projects.
This has been my life for the past five years, basically just living this routine-like life. I realize that there are a couple of people who are able to glide through even this so-called chaotic environment and still be successful.
* They are able to reason through difficult situations.
* They are able to speak confidently at meetings.
* They are able to make sense of a large body of data.
* They are able to focus on what really matters.
* They are able to execute really well.
* Speak with frameworks 
For some other people, they need extra information, extra preparation, and sometimes need to read extensively in order to prepare for those meetings. This is literally my life.I would need more than 8 hours a day to prepare for presentations, regularly 1:1, do my normal day work, resolve issues on Slack, support colleagues, while I maintain a normal.

I started learning how to build stuff and playing with AI, and I realized how AI could be my second brain in meetings, my companion, a true companion. It could essentially catch things my brain or my mind probably wouldn't catch, or reason through things I probably don't have enough context to reason through.

I realized that the smartest of us all are not people who are the greatest with the greatest IQ. There are people with context. Sometimes context really lies with the more senior you are, or sometimes managers tend to have this context. If you are an IC trying to do some work, the last thing you want to do is to get into meaning and not have context.

This is where I thought about this product that basically surfaces risk, surfaces alignment gaps, and essentially helps you ask better questions. It could even tell you what to say in those meetings. A typical situation where you'd use this tool is when you have a very busy day with back-to-back meetings and it's hard to prepare for those meetings.

What if you're able to essentially trust the AI? What if you're able to have AI surface things or bring up stuff for you, or bring stuff to your attention that will be valuable for a conversation in that meeting? What if you had an AI that is able to make you sound intelligent in meetings and have people trust you more? What if you're able to never have to blank out in meetings or just rumble through the meeting without having to create any impact?

This is my belief. My belief is that at some point in the world AI will become our co-worker. AI will become our second brain. AI will help us reason through the most difficult decisions, and AI will make us sound smart and make us facilitate meetings in a better way. Ultimately you can get the promotion you want and build relationships that you want in a very fluid and conversational manner, from one conversation to the other and from a brother to you. I ask that you try this product and let me know what you think.
